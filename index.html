I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at the School of Computer Science, Peking University (PKU) Peking University , working with Prof. Xuanzhe Liu. I received my Ph.D. from at Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications (BUPT) in July 2024 and was fortunate to be advised by Prof. Shangguang Wang and Prof. Mengwei Xu.
